Dalgona Coffee

by Jane Kim

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup whole milk

Directions:

Step 1 Whisk boiling water, instant coffee, and sugar together until coffee is dissolved. Continue to whisk until mixture becomes thick and forms a peak, 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 2 Pour milk into a glass. Pour the frothy coffee on top of the milk.

Cook's Note:

Use instant coffee, water, and sugar in a 1:1:1 ratio. Use the milk of your choice.
